About Online Voting:-

__________ _________ ______

Electronic voting (also known as e-voting) is voting that uses electronic
means to either aid or take care of casting and counting ballots.

Depending on the particular implementation, e-voting may use standalone
electronic voting machines (also called EVM) or computers connected
to the Internet (online voting).
In general, two main types of e-voting can be identified:
  • e-voting which is physically supervised by representatives
    of governmental or independent electoral authorities
  • remote e-voting via the Internet (also called i-voting)
    where the voter submits his or her vote electronically
    to the election authorities, from any location.
